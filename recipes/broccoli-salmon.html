Broccoli salmon pasta

Home

Description

This pasta will change your live. It is so easy to make it will become a weekly dish.

Ingredients

  • Spirelli pasta
  • Salmon
  • Broccoli
  • Parmesan
  • Seasoned butter
  • Onions
  • Garlic Cloves

Steps

  1. Boil you Pasta.
  2. Cut your Broccoli.
  3. Add the broccoli and boil together.
  4. Cutt your salmon, garlic and Onion.
  5. Fry your onion and farlic in a pan.
  6. Take out the broccoli and pasta and keep some boiling water.
  7. Add the seasoned butter, salmon, broccoli and pasta together with the boiling water.
  8. Stir together to keep it nice and creamy.
  9. Put a portion on your plate and finish with some Parmesan.
