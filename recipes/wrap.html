Delicious Wrap

Home

Description

This dish is an all time favorite. You can make your own version depending on your taste. That's what makes it so beautiful.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla wraps
  • Minced meat
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Salad
  • Advocado
  • Tomato
  • Carrot (sliced)

Steps

  1. Cut your Onion and Paprika.
  2. Fry your onion for 1 minute with some olive oil.
  3. Add the paprika for 1 minute.
  4. Add the minced meat and season with some salt and pepper.
  5. Heat your wrap in the oven.
  6. Cut your tomato, Advocado and Carrots.
  7. Take out the wrap and start dressing with everything.
  8. Add some sauce off your linking.
  9. Wrap it up and enjoy!
