Description

This recipe is easy and fast to make. It contains lots of flavours from the asian kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Udon Noodle
  • Soya sauce
  • Salmon
  • Sesame seeds
  • Miso
  • Green onion
  • white sugar
  • Peanut butter
  • Garlic clove

Steps

  1. Fry your salmon.
  2. Boil your Noodles for 3 minutes.
  3. Put the soya sauce, sesame sead, green onion, white sugar, peanut butter and garlic in one pan.
  4. Add some hot water and stir.
  5. Add the salmon and noodles and let it cook a minute.
  6. Take out the noodles and add into your bowl.
  7. Itadakimasu!
